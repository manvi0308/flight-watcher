I was about to pay ₹32,000 for a flight.

Instead, I spent a weekend building a bot that watches prices for me —
and got the same ticket for ₹21,500 when it dropped at 2am.

Here's what it does:
→ Polls Google Flights every 10 minutes
→ Compares price against my target threshold
→ Sends me a WhatsApp alert the moment it drops

The whole stack:
🐍 Python (polling loop + logic)
✈️ SerpAPI (Google Flights data)
📱 Twilio (WhatsApp alerts)
🚂 Railway (free 24/7 deployment)

The part that surprised me most?
The actual "smart" part of the bot is just 15 lines of Python.
Everything else is plumbing — API calls, env vars, deployment config.

Which is kind of the point.
You don't need a complex system to solve a real problem.
You need a clear trigger, a clear action, and something that runs while you sleep.

Total cost to run: $0/month (within free tiers)
Time to build: ~3 hours
Money saved on first use: ₹10,500
